How It Works

Invoice arrives. AI handles it. Your team reviews exceptions.

Three connected steps that move every invoice from inbox to ERP — without manual keying, without chasing approvals, without month-end panic.

01

Invoice Arrives in Any Format

Supplier emails an invoice — PDF, EDI, scanned image, or any format. It lands in the PurchasePlus Invoicing Inbox. No special supplier setup required, no portal login for them to remember.

The Result: Every supplier, on day one. No onboarding friction, no format restrictions.
02

AI Reads, Extracts & Matches

PurchasePlus AI reads every line — supplier, product, quantity, price, tax — with no manual keying. It matches the invoice against the original purchase order and goods receiving note. Three-way match, automated.

The Result: 90% of invoices process straight through. Discrepancies caught before they're paid.
03

Approve or Route — Instantly

Invoices that match go straight through for approval. Discrepancies route automatically to the right person with the exception flagged and the context attached. Your team only touches invoices that need attention.

The Result: Month-end closes on time. AP becomes a controls function, not a data-entry department.
