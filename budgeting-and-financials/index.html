The Budget Reality Check

Your Budget Is a Spreadsheet.
Your Spending Isn't.

Most finance teams set annual budgets in Excel and review actuals once a month — in arrears. By the time the numbers land, the damage is done. Maverick purchasing, unbudgeted orders, and price drift all happen between the spreadsheet and the invoice.

"Month-End Surprises"

Actuals hit your ERP 30 days after the spending happened. There's no way to course-correct in real time — you only find out what went wrong when it's already done.

"No Enforcement at Order Point"

Staff raise purchase orders without knowing if they've exceeded their allocation. Nothing stops them — and the approver has no budget context when signing off.

"Multi-Property Chaos"

The PurchasePlus Advantage

Know your position before you spend. Not after.

Three connected capabilities that turn your budget from a year-end report into an operational guardrail.

01

Commitment-Based Budgeting

PurchasePlus tracks spend from the first requisition — not when the invoice arrives. The moment a PO is approved, it's committed against budget. You see your true position in real time, every day.

The Result: No more month-end surprises. You know the number before it hits the ERP.
02

Budget Thresholds & Approval Routing

Set budget thresholds by department, cost centre, or property. When a purchase order pushes spending over a limit, it routes to the right approver before going to the supplier. Control without micromanagement.

The Result: Overspend becomes an approval decision — not a month-end discovery.
03

Multi-Property & Cost Centre Visibility

Build budgets by property, department, F&B outlet, GL code, or cost centre. Roll them up into a consolidated group view for finance, and down into operational detail for managers on the floor.

The Result: The right visibility at every level of the organisation — from chef to CFO.
