New Integration Live: PurchasePlus + Bundle Connect

End-to-end procurement, now connected to your laundry workflow.

If your operation runs commercial laundry, you already know the gap.

Procurement happens in one system. Linen orders happen in another. Invoices show up in a third. Reconciling them is a Friday-afternoon problem that becomes a Monday-morning headache.

That gap is closed as of today.

PurchasePlus is now natively integrated with Bundle Connect, the laundry-productivity platform used by commercial laundries across accommodation and hospitality.

What the integration does

Five things, all live now for any PurchasePlus customer:

  1. Automatic catalogue sync. Pricing, SKUs, and product lists stay aligned across both platforms without manual updates.
  2. Orders created in Bundle Connect from inside PurchasePlus. No system switching, no re-entering data.
  3. Invoices flow back into PurchasePlus. Faster AP reconciliation, fewer mismatches, cleaner financial workflow.
  4. Role-based purchasing controls across both systems. The right people get the right permissions, end to end.
  5. One-step activation for customer-specific platforms. Built for multi-site groups, franchise networks, and organisations with layered approval structures.

Who it's for

Hotel groups running in-house or contracted laundry. Anyone whose linen line items currently end up reconciled by hand.

What's actually different

The gain isn't software for software's sake. It's a Friday night where the linen order is right, the invoice matches, and nobody has to phone anybody.

How to activate

Already on both platforms? Contact your CSM or email help@purchaseplus.com to get started.
