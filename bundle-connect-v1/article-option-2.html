New Integration Live: PurchasePlus + Bundle Connect

A fully integrated connection between PurchasePlus and Bundle Connect, now available to all customers.

We are pleased to introduce an important upgrade to the PurchasePlus ecosystem: a fully integrated connection with Bundle Connect, now available to all customers across accommodation and hospitality operations.

This integration brings together two powerful platforms to create a unified, automated procurement workflow that removes manual steps, reduces errors, and gives your team more control and visibility from end to end.

If your operation runs commercial laundry, you already know the gap. Procurement happens in one system. Linen orders happen in another. Invoices show up in a third. Reconciling them is a Friday-afternoon problem that becomes a Monday-morning headache.

That gap is closed as of today.

What this means for your laundry

With this integration enabled, your purchasing, ordering, and invoicing processes now work together seamlessly:

  1. Automatic catalogue sync. Your pricing, SKUs, and product lists stay aligned across both platforms without manual updates.
  2. Orders created in Bundle Connect from inside PurchasePlus. No switching systems. No re-entering data. Orders flow instantly into Bundle Connect.
  3. Invoices sent straight back to PurchasePlus. Faster AP reconciliation, fewer mismatches, and a cleaner financial workflow.
  4. Role-based purchasing controls across both systems. The right people get the right permissions, end to end.
  5. One-step activation for customer-specific platforms. Built for multi-site groups, franchise networks, and organisations with layered approval structures.

Why this matters

This integration delivers a more connected, efficient, and reliable supply chain experience. Operators gain:

  • Less admin and fewer manual touchpoints
  • Improved accuracy across orders and invoices
  • Faster fulfilment and clearer communication
  • A single source of truth for purchasing and laundry operations
  • A smoother workflow for teams on the ground and in finance

Who can use it

The integration is available now for all PurchasePlus customers. Hotel groups running in-house or contracted laundry. Anyone whose linen line items currently end up reconciled by hand.

How to activate

Already on both platforms? Contact your CSM or email help@purchaseplus.com to get started.
