Why 30,000+ Suppliers Stay

Seven reasons suppliers call PurchasePlus their easiest channel.

From pricing control to predictable payments, every benefit is designed around the realities of running a supply business.

BENEFIT 01

Your products, in front of buyers actively ordering.

PurchasePlus connects you to the procurement teams at hotels, clubs, healthcare facilities, and more, across Australia, Asia, and beyond. Your catalogue is visible when they're ready to buy.

BENEFIT 02

Update prices once. Every buyer sees it immediately.

Manage your product catalogue and pricing directly in PurchasePlus, manually or via API. No more emailing price lists that get ignored. Your buyers always have accurate, current pricing.

BENEFIT 03

Fewer errors. Fewer disputes. Fewer credits.

When buyers order from your catalogue, they use your exact product codes, descriptions, and units. Orders come through clean, matched to what you actually supply. Less back-and-forth, fewer corrections.

BENEFIT 04

Invoices that match. Payments that arrive.

PurchasePlus buyers do 3-way matching, invoice against PO against receiving note. When your invoice matches the order, it processes straight through. No chasing. No delays caused by discrepancies.

BENEFIT 05

We set you up personally, then stay available.

Our supplier engagement team works directly with each supplier to get your catalogue loaded and your connection live. We don't leave you to figure it out alone. And we're available when you need changes.

BENEFIT 06

Find new buyers you didn't know were looking.

PurchasePlus runs a supplier directory that procurement teams actively search. Being on the platform means being discoverable to buyers who haven't worked with you yet.

BENEFIT 07

Grow alongside 30,000+ peer suppliers.

Join a connected network of established suppliers already transacting through PurchasePlus. Benefit from a platform built on years of supplier feedback, with continuous improvements designed around how your business actually operates.

Since joining PurchasePlus, our order accuracy improved dramatically. Buyers order from our catalogue directly, no more phone calls fixing wrong product codes.
